What is OceanFund?

OceanFund is an investment vehicle where users can stash their extra unused ETH for future returns. The interest rate however, is dynamic and depends on the time for which the money is invested. While you have your invesment deposited in the pool, every time any other user liquidates their funds and extracts their investment from the pool, you get an 0.1% compound interest!

Technology

This is currently out for alpha-testing on Rinkeby. We plan to deploy OceanFund on the MainNet by the end of May. Note: This idea isn't completely new: it's economic model is similar to having a Fixed Deposit. Putting in your money for a long fixed period gives you a positive return. If you break your FD before the time period, you are required to pay a fee to the bank. The returns in OceanFund however are dynamic and depend on the dynamics of the whole system.
